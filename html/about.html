About OEVC (the creator of this webiste)

My name is Omar Velasco, currently I am a graduate student of UNAM who finished his Masters Degree studies in Physics.

Currently (as of 2023), I live in Mexico City, but my place of birth and hometown for 18 years, was Aguascalientes. This town is 3 hours by car from Guadalajara, another big, important city in Mexico.

My passion is Science, computers, watching YouTube and TikTok videos from which I can learn cool stuff and also, learn brand new techniques and tools that can be helpful for computers, programming and science in general.

Here I list a short summary of my life, studies and job career:

  • I was born in a town named Aguascalientes, at the heart of Mexico, in 1994.
  • I lived there for 18 years and in 2012, I moved down to Mexico City to pursue my dreams and my career.
  • I studied a Major in Physics (B. Sc.) at the biggest alma mater in my country (UNAM), in its Faculty of Science. Lasted there studying from 2012 to 2017.
  • I underwent some informal jobs after I finished my undergraduate studies, like being an Assistant Professor and also a helper in a local shop.
  • Later, I got admitted into a graduate program for Masters studies in Science, from 2019 to 2021, again with a specialty on Physics. My area of study was Compact Objects Astrophysics.
  • As I finished my Masters courses, I started working as an intern for a multinational company from China which sells electronic devices and accessories in Mexico and Latam.
  • There, I got promoted to E-commerce Analyst and Director's Assistant as a data analyst, for either the departments of E-commerce and Retail sales.
  • Finally, I had a brief partnership shared with other two friends of mine, when we decided to become holders of a company named "Grupo Fenix Chimex".
